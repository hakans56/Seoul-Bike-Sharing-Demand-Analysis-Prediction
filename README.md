Seoul Bike Sharing Demand Analysis & Prediction

This project analyzes bike rental patterns from the Seoul Bike Sharing system and presents key insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard. The goal is to explore how factors such as season, hour of the day, holidays, and temperature conditions influence bike rental demand and to support demand prediction and operational decision-making.
The dashboard provides a clear overview of rental trends and highlights peak usage periods that can help optimize bike availability and resource planning.

📊 Dashboard Overview

The dashboard summarizes 6 million total bike rentals and presents several key performance indicators and visual analyses:
Key KPIs
Total Bike Rented: 6M
Average Daily Rentals: 16.91K
Peak Hour Rentals: 3M
Holiday Rental Rate: 3.50%
These KPIs provide a quick overview of the system's overall performance and usage patterns.

📈 Insights from the Analysis
Seasonal Demand
Bike rentals are highest during summer and gradually decline through autumn and spring, reaching the lowest levels in winter. This indicates a strong relationship between weather conditions and bike usage.
Hourly Usage Patterns
Demand peaks during evening hours (17:00–23:00), suggesting that many users rely on bike sharing for post-work commuting and leisure activities.
Holiday Impact
The majority of rentals occur on non-holiday days, indicating that bike sharing is primarily used for daily transportation rather than holiday activities.
Monthly Trends
Rental demand increases steadily from early spring, peaks in summer months, and begins to decline towards late autumn and winter.
Temperature Influence
Bike usage is significantly higher during hot and mild temperature conditions, while colder weather reduces demand considerably.

🎯 Project Goal

The primary objective of this project is to demonstrate how data visualization and exploratory analysis can be used to understand demand patterns in urban mobility systems. These insights can help city planners and service providers:
Improve bike availability during peak hours
Optimize seasonal resource allocation
Understand weather-driven demand fluctuations
